# AV-Tech-Sales-Analysis
# Description
This Excel project analyzes AV Tech’s sales performance to uncover insights across regions, products, sales representatives, and revenue trends. The case study focuses on understanding what drives revenue and how sales performance varies across different business dimensions.
Key features include cleaned data, Pivot Tables, visual charts, and an interactive dashboard to support effective business decision-making.


# Project Overview
This project analyzes sales performance data for AV Tech, a technology retail company, to uncover trends in revenue, product performance, and regional sales distribution. The goal is to generate actionable business insights using Excel-based data analysis techniques.
This project is designed to demonstrate data cleaning, analysis, pivot tables, and dashboard storytelling, making it suitable for a junior data analyst / business analyst portfolio.

# Tools Used
•	Microsoft Excel (Excel functions, Pivot Tables, Charts & Dashboard visualizations)

# Key Business Questions Answered

1.	  Which region generates the highest total revenue?

2.	  Which product categories contribute most to overall sales?

3.	  Which individual products are the top revenue drivers?

4.	  Which sales representatives generate the highest sales revenue?

5.	  How is revenue distributed across different regions and products?

6.	  Are higher-priced products always associated with higher revenue?

7.	  What overall sales patterns can be observed across the dataset?



# Key Findings

1️⃣ Product & Category Performance
>   	Electronics-related products generate the highest revenue.
>   	A small number of products contribute a large share of total sales.
>   	Several products show low sales volume and minimal revenue impact.

2️⃣ Regional Sales Performance
>   	The South and East regions record the strongest sales performance.
>   	Other regions show moderate to low revenue contribution.
>     Revenue distribution across regions is uneven, indicating growth opportunities.

3️⃣ Sales Representative Performance
>   	A few top-performing sales representatives account for a significant portion of total revenue.
>   	Performance varies widely among sales reps.
>   	Consistent strategies from high performers could be replicated across the team.

4️⃣ Revenue Distribution & Trends
>   	Revenue is concentrated among specific products, regions, and sales reps.
>   	Higher unit prices do not always guarantee higher revenue; sales volume plays a key role.
>   	Sales trends highlight periods of stronger performance that can inform planning.   

# Conclusion
The analysis shows that AV Tech’s sales performance is driven primarily by high-performing products, regions, and sales representatives. While a small segment contributes most of the revenue, other areas underperform, presenting opportunities for targeted improvement.
Strategic focus on top-performing segments, combined with optimization of weaker areas, can significantly improve overall business performance
